About

Serious enough to read. Self-aware enough not to pretend.

Mega Dump is a meme coin on Ethereum that does not attempt to turn irony into false profundity. The premise is simple: crypto is already full of spectacle, contradiction, and accidental comedy.

MDUMP leans into that reality with cleaner language, clearer design, and a more deliberate presentation. It is branded like an actual project, while staying honest about the category it belongs to.

That balance is the point. Not chaos dressed up as conviction. Just a sharper version of the joke everyone can already see.
